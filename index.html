AI-based chest X-ray solution

The interpretation of chest radiographs is a challenging task, requiring experience and expertise. Studies have reported suboptimal performance in the interpretation of chest radiographs by physicians compared with expert radiologists. However, there is a practical limitation with regard to the full-time availability of expert radiologists, especially for after-hours coverage. Diagnosis through imaging, with its emphasis on identifying subtle patterns that can be hard to detect with the human eye, makes for an ideal use case for AI based disease diagnostic system. We use artificial intelligence to help doctors to make decisions by analyzing x-rays of the patients. Our product classifies 15 different classes of lungs disease using frontal chest x-ray with the help of Deep Neural Network architecture
